Biography

My name is Shubham Sharma and I am currently a final year student pursuing Dual-Degree(B.Tech-M.Tech) in Computer Science and Engineering at Indian Institute of Technology, Kanpur, India. My research is primarily on Propositional Sampling and Counting, Knowledge Compilation, AI and Cyber Security. During my curriculum I have done various projects and courses on these topics and I am fascinated by their research prospects. Currently I am working with Prof. Kuldeep Meel and Prof. Subhajit Roy on Knowledge Compilation. I was fortunate to get to work with Prof. Sandeep Shukla on Blockchains. In the summer of 2017, I interned with Cerebro team at Nutanix India.

Apart from academics, I like to spend my time doing photography, playing games and doing social work for conservation of nature.
